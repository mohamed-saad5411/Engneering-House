About Us

من نحن

Engineering House for Geotechnical and Construction in 2018 under the leadership of Professor Dr. Consulting Engineer/ Walid El-kamash, to be one of the consulting offices specialized in structural engineering with all its details, including soil research and structural designs, in addition to engineering supervision of the entire construction work. Through the experience and capabilities of our engineers, which are characterized by a combination of practical experience and high scientific capabilities, as the office’s leadership has many distinguished lecturers and experts in the field.
